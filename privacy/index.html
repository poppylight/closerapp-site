Closer — Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Introduction

Closer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Closer.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application Closer and its associated services (collectively, our "Service").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Financial Data: Asset names, values, and categories you enter to track your net worth
  • Goal Information: Your financial goals, target amounts, and deadlines
  • Preferences: Currency selection, display preferences, and notification settings

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your real name, email address, or contact information
  • We do NOT collect bank account numbers, credit card details, or login credentials
  • We do NOT connect to your bank or financial institutions
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We use privacy-friendly analytics only (see Third-Party Services below)

How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only

All your financial data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back it up.

Optional iCloud Sync (Pro Feature)

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored in YOUR iCloud account, not ours
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Accessible only to you

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data may only be shared in these limited circumstances:

  • With your consent: If you explicitly choose to share your progress (e.g., using the Share feature)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Third-Party Services

Apple Services

Our app uses the following Apple services:

  • App Store: For processing in-app purchases
  • iCloud (optional): For data backup and sync
  • WidgetKit: For home screen widgets
  • WatchKit: For Apple Watch functionality

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-friendly analytics. TelemetryDeck:

  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT use cookies or track you across apps
  • Only collects anonymous usage data (e.g., which features are used)
  • Is GDPR compliant by design

Learn more: TelemetryDeck Privacy

Stock & Crypto Prices

We use third-party APIs to fetch live prices:

  • Finnhub for stock prices
  • CoinGecko for cryptocurrency prices

These services only receive the stock/crypto symbols you search for. No personal data is sent.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app
  • Delete: Delete all your data by uninstalling the app
  • Export: Export your data (Pro feature)
  • Disable Sync: Turn off iCloud sync at any time

Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app. If you use iCloud sync, your data remains in your iCloud account until you delete it.

Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored using Apple's secure UserDefaults and Keychain
  • Optional Face ID / Touch ID protection
  • iCloud data is encrypted using Apple's security standards

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
